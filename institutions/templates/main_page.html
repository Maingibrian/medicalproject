Our Services

In our services area, we aim to provide a comprehensive and efficient pharmaceutical management system tailored for the unique needs of healthcare institutions and county health workers in Kenya. Our system offers a centralized platform where county health workers can meticulously document the distribution of medical packages to medical institutions. Simultaneously, medical institutions can access this platform to monitor the quantity of medication dispatched to them.
